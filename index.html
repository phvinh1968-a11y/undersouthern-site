About Us

UNDERSOUTHERN is an independent record label founded with the mission of developing young artists and bringing Vietnamese music closer to both domestic and international audiences.

We focus on modern genres, especially Rap / Hip-Hop and contemporary music.

Our label not only supports artists in music production, but also accompanies them in release, promotion, and personal brand building.

Anti-Fraud Policy

At UNDERSOUTHERN, we are committed to protecting artists, creators, and partners from fraudulent activities.

What counts as fraud?
  • Artificial streaming (bot-generated plays)
  • Misleading or inaccurate metadata
  • Unauthorized use of content
  • Musical spam or repeated uploads
Our Actions

✔ Prevention & Monitoring: We actively review streaming data, revenue, and metadata to detect irregularities. ✔ Intervention: Payments may be withheld, services suspended, or collaboration with DSPs and authorities may occur when fraud is suspected. ✔ Responsibility: All artists and partners must provide accurate metadata and cooperate fully in any investigation.
