About Us

UNDERSOUTHERN is an independent record label and global distributor. We empower artists by providing professional distribution, marketing, and brand development, ensuring music reaches audiences worldwide.

Our Artists

Featuring diverse and talented artists across multiple genres, UNDERSOUTHERN is proud to showcase unique voices from around the world.

Available On

Our music catalog is distributed across major digital platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, and more.

Our Mission

At UNDERSOUTHERN, our mission is to discover, nurture, and amplify unique musical values from independent artists. We support artists in building sustainable careers and connect their music with global audiences. With creativity, transparency, and long-term partnership, we act as a bridge between artists and the world.
