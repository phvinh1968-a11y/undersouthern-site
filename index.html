About

UNDERSOUTHERN is an independent record label based in Vietnam, focused on discovering, developing, and distributing music from emerging artists to a global audience.

We work across digital music distribution, artist development, and strategic release planning, ensuring that each release meets international platform standards.

Our network covers major DSPs such as Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, TikTok, and regional platforms worldwide.

UNDERSOUTHERN prioritizes creative freedom, transparency, and long-term collaboration with artists.
