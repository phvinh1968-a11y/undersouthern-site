How long will it take for my music to be available?
Typically, your music will be uploaded online within a period of 1-5 business days, but this depends on the specific service:
  • iTunes / Apple Music: 5 days (Some albums require a manual verification process which may take an additional 1–2 weeks.)
  • Spotify: 2–3 days
  • Amazon: 2–3 days
  • YouTube Music: 2–3 days
  • Deezer: 2–3 days
  • TIDAL: 2–3 days
  • Facebook / Instagram: 2–3 days
  • TikTok: 2–3 days
Can I switch to UNDERSOUTHERN from another distributor?
Of course it's possible, please contact us contact@undersouthern.asia If you need further assistance or have any questions.
