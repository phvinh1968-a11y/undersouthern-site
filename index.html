Content Usage Authorized Use: Any copying, sharing, or commercial use of music distributed by UNDERSOUTHERN must have prior written consent from both the label and the respective artist. Prohibited Actions: Unauthorized reproduction, distribution, or modification of content without consent is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action. 4. Artist Collaboration Mutual Rights and Responsibilities: Artists and UNDERSOUTHERN collaborate under contracts that clearly define the rights, obligations, and revenue shares for both parties. Exclusive Distribution: Music released through UNDERSOUTHERN is exclusively distributed by the label unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing. Promotion and Support: UNDERSOUTHERN commits to promoting artists professionally and ensuring that all distribution activities respect the artist’s creative integrity and legal rights. 5. Limitation of Liability UNDERSOUTHERN is not responsible for unauthorized use of music or content outside the scope of contracts or official platforms. All disputes related to copyright or contract terms will be addressed according to the applicable laws and the terms agreed upon in the artist agreement.
