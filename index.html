UNDERSOUTHERN is an independent record label founded with the goal of developing young artists and bringing Vietnamese music closer to domestic and international audiences. We focus on modern genres, especially Rap HipHop and contemporary music. UNDERSOUTHERN not only supports artists in music production but also accompanies them in releasing, promoting and building their personal brand. With a creative and professional orientation, UNDERSOUTHERN hopes to become a reliable destination for those who are passionate and want to pursue a long-term musical path. © 2025 UNDERSOUTHERN · Independent Music Label
At UNDERSOUTHERN, we are committed to protecting artists, creators, and partners from fraudulent activities such as artificial streaming, misleading metadata, or copyright violations. This policy applies to all End Users (artists, distribution partners, and service providers) with a contractual relationship with UNDERSOUTHERN. Fraudulent practices include but are not limited to: unauthorized use of content, musical spam, inaccurate metadata, and bot-generated streams. Prevention & Monitoring: We actively review streaming data, revenue, and metadata to detect irregularities. All submissions undergo quality checks before release. Intervention: If fraud is suspected, UNDERSOUTHERN may withhold payments, suspend services, or collaborate with DSPs and authorities for further investigation. End User Responsibility: Artists and partners must provide accurate metadata and cooperate fully in any investigation. This policy should be read together with UNDERSOUTHERN’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Updates may be made as required by market, legal, or technological changes. © 2025 UNDERSOUTHERN · Independent Music Label
